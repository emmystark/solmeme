IN A VAST OPEN WORLD

A foreward of guidance – a brief note really – to our future Champions; the Fighters, Visionaries, Kings & Queens of this world…

For many millennia, long after the disappearance of a once great civilization, a new one known to no one began to emerge, first inwardly across 18 regions, building the foundation for itself, and later, outwardly like so many shoots of grass sprouting up through the many cracks of an abandoned city.

Emerging in great numbers and collective strength from the many northern mountains, dense forests, arid desert sands and bioluminescent jungles, a great Ape nation began to awaken and rise up taller, claiming and shaping this new world that we call—THE BANANA KINGDOM.

This world however, is overshadowed. By the ominous light of a moon left fractured by its former occupants. And you, our emerging new champion, will find your new world perpetually rocked by meteorites as a result. Meteorites that contain in their many crags both the fragmented remains of an ancient technology, and scrolls (mostly intact) filled with the knowledge of this once great, former civilization. Apes from all corners of this magical place will bind together alongside you to uncover the answer to a single question – who, or what, was this lost civilization. And what is it trying to tell us…

Immerse into the story and progression of you, our newest champion, alongside all Apekind, and begin to piece together the mysteries from the Sky and this former civilization while building a new world in your image in Quest for the Origin Scrolls; Part One of an upcoming open world MMORPG blockchain game where you’ll push the world and storyline forward. A world where in advancing yourself, you indirectly advance your collective tribe, and the world as a whole in your progression forward down 2 different paths at any one time.

Walk the Path of Exploration to unlock the mysteries of this ancient lost civilization while advancing yourself and your world through discovery, technology and engineering, or walk the Path of the Warrior and battle to expand your version of civilization, your influence, and your control over the Kingdom’s 18 regions and it’s resources. You may switch between, and advance in either path, at any time. So, where do we begin… Ah yes, lets start with the path of exploration…

THE PATH OF EXPLORATION

REVEAL MORE

FINDING ORIGIN SCROLLS & UNLOCKING LAND STRUCTURES, WEAPONS, GEAR, MAPS, TECHNOLOGY AND MORE

A treasure trove of meteorites to mine, and Scroll knowledge to uncover is only the beginning for those who walk the Path of Exploration. Join others on the same path and begin a world-wide quest to piece together the remains of the Sky Pillars, and, a mysterious scroll - know amongst Apes as the Sky Scroll. A scroll that will unlock both a technology unlike any other the Apes have ever seen... and take them to an unexpected place of unparalleled possibility. A place that also answers the looming question on the minds of all Apes: What happened to this lost civilization...

THE GUILDS  

ECONOMY

EXCLUSIVE CRAFTING, BUILDING AND SERVICES

  • Land Structure Crafting is Exclusive to the Builder Class
  • Shard Element Enhancements are Exclusive to the Oracle Class

REVEAL MORE

Builders and Oracles in any Region are automatically members of their respective guilds and these guild memberships are automatic licenses. The Kingdom's Builders are the only ones allowed to create structural buildings and land defenses which can be sold to any other player, or used for their tribe, village etc. Similarly, Oracles have the exclusive license to craft weapons and gear enhanced with Shard magic. In addition, Builders can offer restoration services for defensive structures and Oracles can offer one time enhancement services via their Guild's storefronts.
